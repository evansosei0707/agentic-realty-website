# Agentic Realty — Website Rebrand & Conversion Rebuild

This site started as the "Sankofield" prototype. It has been rebranded to
**Agentic Realty** (company), keeping **Ama** as the AI agent persona, and
rebuilt as a conversion-first marketing site. Stack is unchanged: React 19 +
TanStack Router + Tailwind v4 + GSAP.

## What changed

- **Brand palette → "Modern Wealth"** (emerald + gold on near-black). All
  tokens live in `src/styles.css`. The old acid lime `#d1fe17` is fully
  removed as the lead color and demoted to `--accent-lime` (`#c9f24d`),
  reserved for in-product "automation alive" sparks only.
  - Brand emerald: `#34d8a0` (dark) / `#0a6b4f` (light, AA-legible).
  - Gold accent: `#e8c275`. Canvas: `#0a0f0c`.
- **Typography:** added **Fraunces** (display serif) for headlines over
  **Geist** (body/UI). Used via the `font-display` utility.
- **Logo:** interim typeset lockup (emerald "A" monogram + wordmark) in
  `src/components/landing/primitives/logo.tsx` + `public/favicon.svg`.
  Replace with the final logo art when it lands (see designer brief below).
- **CTAs are now wired.** New `DemoCTA` / `WhatsAppCTA` primitives
  (`src/components/landing/primitives/cta.tsx`) + event tracking
  (`src/lib/analytics.ts`). Targets live in `src/lib/site.ts`.
- **New conversion sections (Phase B):** problem/agitation, CRM-by-text
  spotlight, proof ("we tried to break her"), an interactive ROI calculator,
  an Ama-vs-hiring comparison table, a 5-day speed-to-live timeline, a
  risk-reversal/guarantee band, a founder/"Made in Accra" block, and a final
  CTA. All consume copy from `src/lib/content.ts`.
- **Honest social proof:** the fake "trusted by meQasa/Jiji/..." logo row was
  removed (false-endorsement risk) and replaced with verifiable trust points.

## Honesty guardrails honored (do not regress)

Only `LIVE` features are claimed. No fake client logos, no projected numbers
shown as pilot results (ROI is labelled illustrative), no "certified DPA
compliant", and the "sounds human" framing avoids the unqualified "leads
can't tell it's AI" claim (disclosure policy is per-client).

## Logo designer brief

Must work single-color in **gold AND green**, read on both a near-black and an
off-white background, and survive small/print. No neon. Any such mark drops
into the Modern Wealth palette without rework.
